Pan-Seared Scallops with Lemon Butter 🐚

Prep: 15 mins | ♨️ Calories: 280 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • The Scallops: 10-12 large sea scallops (patted dry).
  • The Sear: 1 tbsp olive oil and 1 tbsp unsalted butter.
  • The Sauce: 2 tbsp butter, 2 cloves minced garlic, and fresh lemon juice.
  • Herbs: Freshly chopped parsley.
  • Seasoning: Coarse sea salt and freshly cracked black pepper.

Instructions

    1. Remove the side muscle from the scallops if attached. Pat them completely dry with paper towels (this is key for a good crust).
    2. Season the scallops with salt and pepper right before cooking.
    3. Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet over high heat until smoking hot.
    4. Place scallops in the pan. Sear for 2 minutes on one side until a golden-brown crust forms.
    5. Flip and cook for only 1 more minute. Remove from the pan immediately so they don't overcook.
    6. In the same pan, melt the remaining butter with garlic and lemon juice. Drizzle over the scallops and garnish with parsley.
