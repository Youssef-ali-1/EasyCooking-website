Spaghetti Carbonara

Prep: 15 mins | ♨️ Calories: 550 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 250g high-quality Italian Spaghetti.
  • The Meat: 150g premium smoked beef bacon, diced.
  • Cheese: 1 cup of freshly grated Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese.
  • Eggs: 3 large fresh egg yolks and 1 whole egg.
  • Seasoning: A generous amount of freshly cracked black pepper (no salt needed, the cheese and bacon are salty enough).

Instructions

    1. Boil the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until perfectly al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
    2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, whole egg, grated cheese, and plenty of black pepper to create a thick paste.
    3. Place the diced beef bacon in a cold skillet, then turn the heat to medium. Cook until crisp and the fat has rendered (about 5-7 minutes).
    4. Turn off the heat! Add the hot, drained spaghetti directly to the skillet and toss it well with the bacon and its oils.
    5. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta. Toss vigorously and continuously, adding a splash of the hot pasta water to create a silky, creamy sauce (the residual heat cooks the eggs without scrambling them).
    6. Serve immediately, garnished with an extra dusting of cheese and more black pepper.
