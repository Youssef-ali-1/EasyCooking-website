Prime Ribeye Steak with Herb Butter 🥩

Prep: 25 mins | ♨️ Calories: 820 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • The Beef: 2 Prime Ribeye steaks (1.5 inches thick).
  • The Herb Butter: 3 tbsp butter, fresh rosemary, and thyme sprigs.
  • Aromatics: 4 cloves of smashed garlic.
  • Seasoning: Sea salt and coarsely ground black pepper.
  • Oil: 1 tbsp high-heat cooking oil.

Instructions

    1. Bring the steaks to room temperature for 30 minutes before cooking. Pat them dry and season heavily.
    2. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until very hot. Add the oil.
    3. Sear the steaks for 3-4 minutes per side to develop a deep, dark crust.
    4. Reduce heat to medium. Add the butter, garlic, and fresh herbs to the pan.
    5. Tilt the skillet and use a spoon to continuously baste the melted herb butter over the steaks for 2 more minutes.
    6. Transfer the steaks to a board and let them rest for 10 minutes before slicing to keep the juices inside.
