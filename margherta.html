CLASSIC MARGHERITA PIZZA

Prep: 25 mins | ♨️ Calories: 450 kcal

Ingredients

  • Dough: 250g of high-quality pizza dough, rolled out to a 12-inch circle.
  • Sauce: 3 tablespoons of San Marzano tomato sauce.
  • Cheese: 100g of fresh Mozzarella di Bufala, sliced.
  • Herbs: 5-6 fresh Basil leaves for an aromatic touch.
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon of Extra Virgin Olive oil.
  • Seasoning: A pinch of sea salt to taste.

Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to the maximum temperature (ideally 250°C-300°C).
    2. Stretch the dough on a floured surface into a perfect 12-inch circle.
    3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving 1 cm for the crust.
    4. Add the fresh mozzarella slices and a drizzle of olive oil.
    5. Bake for 7-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves immediately.
