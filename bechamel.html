Macaroni béchamel

Prep: 45 mins | ♨️ Calories: 600 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 500g Penne pasta, boiled al dente.
  • Meat Filling: 500g ground beef with minced onions and spices.
  • Béchamel Sauce: 1 liter milk, 5 tbsp flour, and 4 tbsp butter.
  • Spices: A pinch of nutmeg, salt, and black pepper.
  • Topping: 1 cup of shredded Mozzarella or Parmesan cheese.
  • Binding: 1 whisked egg (optional for the golden crust).

Instructions

    1. Cook the penne pasta in salted boiling water until it's just firm (al dente).
    2. Sauté the onions and ground beef in a pan until browned, then season with salt and pepper.
    3. Prepare the Béchamel: Melt butter, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk until thick and creamy.
    4. Mix a small portion of the Béchamel with the cooked pasta.
    5. In a baking dish, layer half the pasta, add the meat filling, then the remaining pasta.
    6. Pour the rest of the Béchamel on top, sprinkle cheese, and bake at 200°C for 30 minutes.
