Raspberry Chocolate Cups

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Whipped Cream
  • Raspberries

Raspberry Chocolate Cups Recipe

  1. Melt dark chocolate using a double boiler or microwave.
  2. Line a muffin tray with paper cups.
  3. Spoon a small amount of melted chocolate into each cup and spread it along the sides.
  4. Place the tray in the refrigerator for 10–15 minutes until the chocolate sets.
  5. Add a layer of fresh raspberries or raspberry filling.
  6. Cover with another layer of melted chocolate.
  7. Chill again until fully set.
  8. Remove from the cups and serve.

nutrition facts

nutrient amount
Dark Chocolate 200 g
Fresh Raspberries 1 cup
Raspberry Filling / Jam (optional) 1/2 cup
Muffin Cups 6–8 pieces
