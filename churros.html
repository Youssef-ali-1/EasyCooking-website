Churros

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Churros Recipe

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, butter, sugar, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and add flour. Stir until the mixture forms a dough.
  3. Let the dough cool slightly, then mix in eggs and vanilla extract until smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  5. Pipe the dough into hot oil using a piping bag with a star tip.
  6. Fry until golden brown and crispy, then remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Roll the churros in a mixture of sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Serve warm with chocolate sauce if desired.

nutritional facts

nutrient amount
Water 1 cup
Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Flour 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Oil (for frying) As needed
