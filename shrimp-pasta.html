Shrimp & Tomato Spaghetti

Prep: 20 mins | ♨️ Calories: 450 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 400g Italian Spaghetti.
  • The Seafood: 400g large shrimp, peeled and deveined.
  • Tomatoes: 2 cups of fresh cherry tomatoes, halved.
  • Flavor Base: 4 cloves of garlic (minced) and a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  • The Sauce: 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, 2 tbsp butter, and a splash of fresh lemon juice.
  • Garnish: Fresh basil leaves and a light sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.

Instructions

    1. Boil the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
    2. Season the shrimp with salt, black pepper, and a pinch of paprika.
    3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the shrimp for 1-2 minutes per side until pink, then remove and set aside.
    4. In the same skillet, melt the butter. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    5. Toss in the cherry tomatoes and cook for 4-5 minutes until they blister and release their sweet juices. Add a splash of lemon juice.
    6. Add the cooked spaghetti and the seared shrimp back into the skillet. Pour in a little pasta water and toss everything together until a light, glossy sauce forms.
    7. Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately!
