Chocolate Layer Cake Squares

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Whipped Cream

Chocolate Layer Cake Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease two round cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract, then mix until smooth.
  4. Slowly add hot water or coffee and stir well.
  5. Divide the batter evenly between the pans.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes, then let the cakes cool completely.
  7. Prepare chocolate frosting by mixing butter, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and milk.
  8. Spread frosting between the layers and cover the entire cake.
  9. Decorate as desired and serve.
