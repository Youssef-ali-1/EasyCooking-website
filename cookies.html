Double Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips

Double Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips until evenly distributed.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking tray.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes, then let them cool before serving.

nutritional facts

nutrient amount
Flour 1 1/2 cups
Cocoa Powder 1/2 cup
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Butter (softened) 1/2 cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
White Sugar 1/4 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Chocolate Chips 1 cup
