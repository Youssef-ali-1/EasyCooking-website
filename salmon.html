Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

Prep: 20 mins | ♨️ Calories: 420 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • The Fish: 2 fresh Salmon fillets (about 200g each).
  • The Veggies: 1 bunch of fresh Asparagus, tough ends trimmed.
  • The Marinade: 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil and 3 cloves of minced garlic.
  • Citrus: 1 fresh Lemon (half juiced, half sliced for garnish).
  • Seasoning: Coarse sea salt, freshly cracked black pepper, and a pinch of paprika.
  • Herbs: Fresh dill or parsley for a pop of green.

Instructions

    1. Preheat your grill or a heavy cast-iron grill pan over medium-high heat.
    2. Toss the trimmed asparagus with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper until well coated.
    3. Rub the salmon fillets with the remaining olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, paprika, salt, and pepper.
    4. Place the salmon on the grill. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side until it develops beautiful grill marks and flakes easily with a fork.
    5. Grill the asparagus alongside the salmon for 3-5 minutes, turning occasionally until tender and slightly charred.
    6. Serve the hot salmon and asparagus together, garnished with fresh lemon slices and a sprinkle of dill.
