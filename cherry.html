Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Biscuits
  • Butter
  • Strawberries

instruction

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix them with melted butter.
  2. Press the mixture into the bottom of a cake pan to form the crust.
  3. Beat cream cheese with sugar and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Add heavy cream and mix well until creamy.
  5. Pour the mixture over the biscuit crust.
  6. Refrigerate for 4–6 hours until set.
  7. Top with strawberry sauce before serving.

nutritional facts

nutrient amount
Biscuits 200 g
Melted Butter 100 g
Cream Cheese 400 g
Sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Strawberry Sauce 1/2 cup
