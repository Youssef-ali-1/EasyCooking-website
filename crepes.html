Crepes with Fruits

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Fruits

Crepes with Fruits Recipe

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, milk, eggs, sugar, and a pinch of salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  3. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and swirl to spread evenly.
  4. Cook for 1–2 minutes on each side until lightly golden.
  5. Repeat with the remaining batter to make more crepes.
  6. Fill each crepe with fresh fruits like strawberries, bananas, or berries.
  7. Drizzle with honey, chocolate sauce, or powdered sugar.
  8. Fold or roll the crepes and serve immediately.

nutrition

nutrient amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 1/2 cups
Eggs 2
Sugar 1 tbsp
Salt A pinch
Butter For greasing
Fresh Fruits (strawberries, bananas, berries) As desired
Honey / Chocolate Sauce / Powdered Sugar As desired
