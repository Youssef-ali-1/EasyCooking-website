Classic Chicken Parmesan with Spaghetti 🍗

Prep: 35 mins | ♨️ Calories: 680 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • The Chicken: 2 boneless chicken breasts, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness.
  • The Breading: 1/2 cup flour, 1 egg (beaten), and 1 cup panko breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
  • The Pasta: 200g Italian Spaghetti.
  • The Topping: 1 cup Marinara sauce and 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese.
  • Cooking: Olive oil for frying and fresh basil for garnish.

Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
    2. Dredge chicken in flour, dip in egg, then coat with the breadcrumb-parmesan mixture.
    3. Pan-fry the chicken in olive oil for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
    4. Place chicken in a baking dish. Top with marinara sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella.
    5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
    6. Serve the crispy chicken over the hot spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil leaves.
