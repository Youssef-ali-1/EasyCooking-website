Mini Fruit Tart

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Pastry Cream
  • Fruits

Mini Fruit Tart Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, butter, sugar, and an egg to form a dough.
  3. Press the dough into small tart molds.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden, then let them cool.
  5. Prepare custard by heating milk and mixing it with sugar, egg yolks, and cornstarch.
  6. Cook the custard until thick, then let it cool.
  7. Fill each tart shell with custard.
  8. Top with fresh fruits like strawberries, kiwi, and blueberries.
  9. Brush with a light glaze if desired and serve.

nutrition facts

nutrient amount
Milk 2 cups
Sugar 1/2 cup
Egg Yolks 2
Cornstarch 2 tbsp
Fresh Fruits (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries) As desired
Glaze (optional) As needed
