Creamy Mushroom Pappardelle

Prep: 25 mins | ♨️ Calories: 480 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 300g dried or fresh Pappardelle pasta.
  • Mushrooms: 300g mixed mushrooms (Cremini and Shiitake), thinly sliced.
  • The Base: 2 cloves of garlic (minced) and 1 shallot (finely chopped).
  • Cream & Butter: 3/4 cup of heavy cream and 2 tbsp unsalted butter.
  • Cheese: 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.
  • Finishing Touch: Fresh parsley, salt, black pepper, and a few drops of Truffle oil (optional).

Instructions

    1. Cook the Pappardelle in a large pot of generously salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
    2. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until beautifully browned (about 6-8 minutes).
    3. Reduce the heat to medium. Add the minced garlic and shallot, stirring for 1 minute until fragrant.
    4. Pour in the heavy cream and let it gently simmer for 2-3 minutes to thicken slightly.
    5. Add the cooked Pappardelle directly into the skillet along with a splash of the reserved pasta water.
    6. Toss gently, gradually adding the grated Parmigiano-Reggiano until the sauce elegantly coats the wide pasta ribbons.
    7. Garnish with fresh parsley, freshly cracked black pepper, and a drizzle of truffle oil before serving.
