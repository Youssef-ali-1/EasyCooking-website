Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Chocolate Brownies Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together, then let it cool slightly.
  3. In a bowl, whisk sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the melted chocolate mixture and mix well.
  5. Stir in flour, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for 20–25 minutes until the center is set.
  8. Let it cool before cutting into squares and serving.

nutritional facts

nutrient amount
Butter 1/2 cup
Dark Chocolate 200 g
Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Flour 3/4 cup
Cocoa Powder 1/4 cup
Salt A pinch
