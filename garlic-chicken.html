Creamy Garlic Chicken

Prep: 30 mins | ♨️ Calories: 450 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts.
  • The Sauce: 1 cup of heavy whipping cream.
  • Garlic: 4 cloves of fresh garlic, minced.
  • Cheese: 1/2 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Flavor: 1 tablespoon of fresh lemon juice and lemon slices.
  • Oil & Butter: 1 tbsp olive oil and 1 tbsp unsalted butter.
  • Garnish: Fresh chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper.

Instructions

    1. Season the chicken breasts generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
    2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken for 5-7 minutes per side until golden brown, then set aside.
    3. In the same skillet, melt the butter and sauté the minced garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    4. Lower the heat, pour in the heavy cream and lemon juice, and let it simmer gently for 2 minutes.
    5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.
    6. Return the chicken to the skillet, coat it with the creamy sauce, and simmer for another 3 minutes.
    7. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon slices before serving warm.
