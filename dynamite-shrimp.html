Signature Dynamite Shrimp 🍤

Prep: 20 mins | ♨️ Calories: 420 kcal | 🍲 Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • The Shrimp: 300g medium-sized shrimp, peeled and deveined.
  • The Coating: 1/2 cup cornstarch, 1 beaten egg, and a pinch of salt/pepper.
  • The Sauce: 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 2 tbsp Sriracha, 1 tbsp honey, and 1 tsp garlic powder.
  • Frying: Vegetable oil for deep frying.
  • Garnish: Fresh spring onions (chopped) and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.

Instructions

    1. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Sriracha, honey, and garlic powder to make the signature sauce.
    2. Dip each shrimp into the beaten egg, then coat thoroughly with the seasoned cornstarch.
    3. Heat oil in a pan to 180°C. Fry the shrimp in batches for 3 minutes until golden and crispy.
    4. Drain the shrimp on paper towels for a minute, then toss them gently in the dynamite sauce.
    5. Serve immediately in a tall glass or bowl, topped with spring onions.
